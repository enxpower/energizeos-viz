📘 EnergizeOS™ Strategy Modules – Theory

1️⃣ TOU – Time-of-Use Optimization

Principle: exploit tariff differentials (peak/valley) to plan low-price charging and high-price discharging, minimizing total bill.

原理：利用峰、平、谷价差优化储能充放电计划，低价充电、高价放电，降低总体电费。

Scenarios:
  • Commercial/industrial customers with expensive peak tariffs
  • Enough capacity to bridge peak/valley
Key logic:
  • Preset charge/discharge windows & power limits
  • Monitor SOC / price / load
  • Discharge in peak when SOC ≥ threshold
  • Charge in valley when SOC ≤ threshold
应用场景：
  • 商业/工业高峰电价昂贵
  • 储能容量足以跨越高低谷
关键逻辑：
  • 预设充/放电时间窗与功率限制
  • 实时监测 SOC、电价、负载
  • 进入高价且 SOC 满足阈值时放电
  • 进入低价且 SOC 低于阈值时充电

2️⃣ Demand Charge Management (DCM)

Principle: discharge before a demand peak forms to cap the billed 15/30-minute average demand (kW).

原理：通过实时监测负载功率，预测将超出需量阈值时自动放电削峰，降低最大需量值。

Scenarios:
  • High demand-charge portion in bill
  • Load profile shows sharp peaks
Key logic:
  • Compute rolling 15/30-min averages
  • Trigger discharge on forecast/avg exceedance
  • Adjust power/duration to avoid early SOC depletion
  • Coordinate with TOU to avoid conflicts
应用场景：
  • 需量费占比高
  • 用电曲线存在瞬时高峰
关键逻辑：
  • 持续计算 15/30 分钟需量预测
  • 预计超标立即触发 BESS 放电
  • 出力/时长动态调整，避免 SOC 过早耗尽
  • 可与 TOU 联动避免冲突

3️⃣ Grid-Tied / Islanded Transition

Principle: on abnormal voltage/frequency, island safely; re-close after interlocks (C1–C7) and sync.

原理：电压/频率偏离标准时安全转离网，市电恢复且满足互锁条件后并网。

Scenarios:
  • Microgrids / critical loads
  • Unstable grids
Key logic:
  • Dual-channel sensing (relay + meter)
  • EMS trip command to anti-islanding relay
  • Re-close only with C1–C7 satisfied and zero-export
应用场景：
  • 微电网、关键负载应急供电
  • 电网不稳定/故障频繁
关键逻辑：
  • 双通道感知（继电器+电表）
  • EMS 下发跳闸控制
  • 并网需满足 C1–C7 并先同步相位与零逆流

4️⃣ Diesel Generator Coordination

Principle: keep DG in optimal load band; BESS absorbs low-load and assists at high-load to cut fuel/O&M.

原理：协调柴油机与储能出力，削减低负载运行时间并在高负载时辅助，降低油耗与维护成本。

Key logic:
  • DG start/stop thresholds
  • Below low threshold: BESS takes over and stops DG
  • Above high threshold or low SOC: start DG
  • Maintain 50–80% rated load
关键逻辑：
  • 设定柴油机启停功率阈值
  • 低于低阈值：BESS 接管并停机
  • 超过高阈/或 SOC 低：启机
  • 维持 50–80% 额定功率区间

5️⃣ AI-Driven Forecast & Control

Principle: use historical data, weather, and plans to forecast load/PV and pre-optimize dispatch.

原理：利用历史负载、天气、生产计划等信息预测未来曲线，提前优化调度。

Key logic:
  • Time-series models (e.g., LSTM/Prophet)
  • Combine tariffs & SOC constraints to produce optimal schedule
  • Rolling retrain to improve accuracy
  • Fewer manual interventions
关键逻辑：
  • 时间序列模型（LSTM、Prophet 等）
  • 结合电价与 SOC 约束生成最优调度
  • 持续滚动更新模型
  • 减少人工干预

6️⃣ PV Self-Consumption Maximization

Principle: prioritize PV to load; store surplus in BESS; avoid export.

原理：优先用光伏满足负载，多余存入 BESS，避免上网或反送，提升自用率。

Key logic:
  • Monitor PV, load, SOC
  • Surplus PV → charge BESS
  • Load↑ and PV不足 → BESS discharge
  • Coordinate with Zero-Export
关键逻辑：
  • 实时监测光伏、负载与 SOC
  • 光伏余量优先充电
  • 负载上升且光伏不足时由 BESS 补足
  • 与 Zero-Export 配合确保不外送

7️⃣ Zero Export Enforcement

Principle: hold PCC ≤ 0 via fast PCS regulation (100–200 ms typical).

原理：通过快速调节 PCS 确保并网点功率方向 ≤ 0（典型 100–200ms）。

Key logic:
  • Metering by eGauge / SEL-735
  • Detect export tendency and react quickly
  • Coordinate with PV self-use and TOU
关键逻辑：
  • eGauge/SEL-735 监测并网点功率
  • 检测到外送趋势立即调节
  • 与光伏自用、TOU 联动防冲突
